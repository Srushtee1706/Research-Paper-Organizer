Our Story

Founded in 2023, Research Paper Organizer was born out of a simple need: to bring order to the chaos of academic research. As students and researchers, we struggled with managing countless papers, citations, and notes. We built this platform to be the solution we always wished we had‚Äîa clean, intuitive, and powerful tool to streamline the entire research process.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower researchers, students, and academics worldwide by providing a state-of-the-art platform that simplifies paper management and citation generation. We believe that technology should be a tool for progress, and we are committed to helping the next generation of innovators focus on their work, not on the administrative burden of research.
